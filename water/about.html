About-Us

Our Story

Founded in 2022 by Puja Barman, JalSeva was born out of a simple yet powerful vision — to make clean and safe drinking water accessible to everyone, everywhere. Starting from Cooch Behar, a small town with big challenges, Puja saw the daily struggles people faced just to get drinking water. She believed technology could bridge this gap. What started as a small initiative has now grown into a tech-driven water supply network that connects customers to certified water distributors through an easy-to-use mobile app. Whether you’re in the heart of the city or in a rural village, JalSeva brings trusted brands like Bisleri and Bailley to your doorstep within 24 to 48 hours.

Our Goal

At JalSeva, our goal is simple — to make clean, safe, and reliable drinking water available to every household, workplace, and event, without hassle. We aim to eliminate the daily struggle of accessing pure water by offering an easy, transparent, and tech-enabled platform. With just a few taps, customers can schedule deliveries of trusted ISI-certified brands — ensuring health and convenience go hand in hand. Our long-term vision goes beyond delivery. We are building a sustainable water ecosystem that empowers local distributors, reduces plastic waste, and supports smart water usage across India. Whether you are in an urban city or a remote village, JalSeva is committed to being your most reliable partner in hydration — delivering water that’s not just safe, but also responsibly sourced and efficiently managed.

Our Mission

At JalSeva, our mission is to ensure that every individual has easy access to clean, affordable, and safe drinking water — anytime, anywhere. We strive to bridge the gap between trusted water suppliers and customers through innovation, transparency, and seamless service. By leveraging technology, we make water delivery smarter, faster, and more accessible — reaching even the most remote parts of the country.

  • Our mission is driven by three core values: Accessibility – Bringing clean water to every doorstep, whether urban or rural.
  • Trust – Partnering only with certified, high-quality water brands.
  • Sustainability – Promoting responsible water use and eco-friendly practices.
